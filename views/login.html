Quiz About Web Application Development

Welcome to the online test section. You will compete with other contestants on knowledge related to Web development. There are a total of ten questions in the test. The rules are as follows

1. The player who clicks the right answer faster will win the round and gets 2 points, while the opponent gets 0 points.

2. Player who clicks a wrong answer will get 0 points, and the opponent will get 1 point instead.

3. In the end, the winner is the player with more points.

4. Each question has a 30-second answering time. If neither party answers the question within the specified time, both will receive 0 points.
